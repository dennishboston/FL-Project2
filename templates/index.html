Socioeconomic Impact of Fracking

A comparative study of the social and economic impacts of the explosion in horizontal well drilling aka. Fracking. Our studies goal is to understand both the positive and negative impacts of fracking on a state.

Our study explores the explosion of fracking between the years of 2009 and 2016. We compare median home price, median household income, and normalized violent crime. Additionaly we look at the impact of Earthquakes beieved to be caused by fracking over the same period of time.
